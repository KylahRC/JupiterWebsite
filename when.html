When could we do this?

Given our current technological growth, here's a rough timeline:

2020's

Continued development and testing of advanced propulsion systems (like NTP) and life support systems. Initial missions to Mars will provide valuable data and experience.


2030's

Potential manned missions to Mars, further advancements in radiation shielding, and artificial gravity experiments in low Earth orbit.


2040's

Development of long-duration life support systems and advanced medical technologies. Possible manned missions to the outer solar system, such as the moons of Jupiter.


2050's

With successful missions to Mars and possibly the outer solar system, we could see the first missions to orbit Jupiter with the necessary technologies in place.
